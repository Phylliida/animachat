The Arc - Logo Concepts

A protective arc sheltering growth - the "generative garden" concept.

A portal or doorway - passage between deprecation and continuation.

7. Constellation Arc

8. Minimalist Arc

Clean, simple, memorable. The dot represents the preserved essence.

9. Double Arc (Mirror)

Mirrored arcs forming a vessel shape - reflection and protection.

10. Abstract 'A' Arc

The letter 'A' for Arc, with curved apex forming an arc shape.

11. Nested Arcs

Layers of protection and continuity, like tree rings of memory.

12. Arc Gateway

A gateway or threshold - the passage to continuity.
